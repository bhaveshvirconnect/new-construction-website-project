Railway Construction

We play a key role in expanding and modernizing India's railway network. Our railway construction capabilities cover new rail lines, gauge conversion, track laying, earthwork, station development, and allied civil structures such as platforms, foot overbridges, and subways. With expertise in both conventional rail and high-speed rail corridors, we ensure compliance with Indian Railways' specifications while meeting the nation's rising demand for faster and safer rail transport.

Renewable Energy Infrastructure

Recognizing the future lies in clean energy, we contribute to India's sustainable development through civil infrastructure for renewable energy projects. Our scope includes site development, foundation works for solar panels and wind turbines, internal roads, stormwater drainage, fencing, control rooms, and equipment installation support. With a focus on green engineering practices and project scalability, we support our clients in transitioning toward a zero-carbon future.

Metro Construction

Flyover Construction

Flyovers are critical in decongesting urban road networks and enhancing commuter experience. Our flyover construction vertical encompasses turnkey execution-from foundation and piling to girder launching and deck slab concreting. Whether it's single-span or multi-span, curved or straight, we handle complex traffic conditions and space constraints to deliver structures that are functional, long-lasting, and architecturally refined. Each flyover we build is a step toward a smarter, faster city.
